*   You add **~4.2 Events per Month** (to reach 50/year).
*   Each month, you add **416 minutes** of new video.
*   The billing grows cumulatively as you store more video.

## Summary of Year 1 Totals
*   **Legacy (540p)**: Starts at $1, ends at $12. Total Spend: **~$78**.
*   **Pro (1080p)**: Starts at $13, ends at $27.50. Total Spend: **~$247**.
    *   *Includes $12.50/mo encoded input costs.*
*   **Premium (4K)**: Starts at $45, ends at $90. Total Spend: **~$811**.
    *   *Includes $41.60/mo encoded input costs.*

## Conclusions
1.  **4K is manageable**: Even at the *end* of the year, your bill is under $100/month.
2.  **1080p is trivial**: Your bill is basically the cost of Netflix.
3.  **Legacy is pointless**: Saving $15/month isn't worth a broken product.

## Recommendation
**Go with 1080p (Pro).** It keeps your Year 1 costs under $250 total.
**Crucial Note**: This assumes typical "Plus" tier pricing. Mux billing is exact, so actual cents may vary slightly.
